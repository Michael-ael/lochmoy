Our Services

Passion & Expertise

At Lochmoy, our array of services reflects the depth of our passion for wine and the breadth of our expertise. From the carefully curated e-commerce platform of Lochmoy Uncorked, where wine enthusiasts can acquire premium vintages with ease, to the personalized experiences offered by Lochmoy Wrapped, which transforms events into celebrations of fine wine. Our expert team brings together sommeliers, market strategists, and event planners, all dedicated to providing unparalleled service. Whether guiding a collector to a prudent investment or orchestrating an event that speaks volumes, we pour our knowledge, enthusiasm, and discerning eye for quality into every aspect of what we do.

Our Legacy and Core Values

We Live & Breath Fine Wine

Spanning over a decade in the fine wine realm, Lochmoy has become synonymous with integrity, excellence, and unwavering commitment to our clients' success. Our journey began with a vision to merge time-honored tradition with forward-thinking investment strategies, cultivating a trusted brand that stands as a pillar of stability in the dynamic wine market. We believe in fostering sustainable growth through meticulous market analysis, ensuring that every decision is informed, every investment sound, and every client partnership built on a solid foundation of mutual trust and shared ambition.
